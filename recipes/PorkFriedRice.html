Pork Fried Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Butter
  • 1 (6 ounce) boneless pork loin chop, cut into small pieces
  • ¼ cup chopped carrot
  • ¼ cup chopped broccoli
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup cold cooked rice
  • ¼ cup frozen peas
  • 1 ½ tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground ginger

How to make Pork Fried rice

  1. Step 1

    2. Melt butter in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir pork, carrot, broccoli, peas, and green onion in melted butter until pork is cooked through, 7 to 10 minutes. Remove pork mixture to a bowl and return skillet to medium heat.

  2. Step 2

    3. Scramble egg in the skillet until completely set. Return the pork mixture to the skillet. Stir rice, peas, soy sauce, garlic powder, and ground ginger into the pork mixture; cook and stir until heated through, 7 to 10 minutes.
